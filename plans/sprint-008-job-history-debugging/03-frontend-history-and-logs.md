# 03 - Frontend History And Logs

## Goal

Make it easy to find recent jobs and inspect logs from the UI.

## Tasks

- Add recent jobs list to home page.
- Link each recent job to its progress page.
- Add logs panel to job page.
- Refresh logs while job is non-terminal.

## Acceptance Criteria

- User can open recent jobs without browsing `storage/jobs`.
- Failed jobs show useful logs on the job page.
